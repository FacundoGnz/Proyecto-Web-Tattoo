YOUR
PRECIOUS

EAT SUPER WELL BEFORE THE SESSION

At least 1 hour before. And please, also take with you some snacks, chocolates and water, it’s very important!

WEAR CORRECT CLOTHES

Especially if the area for your tattoo is under Your breast, on your ribs, etc. You have to be prepared for the moment where you need to take your clothes off and put some special stickers for your nipples, which means it is going to be comfortable for you and me as well.

DO NOT WEAR TIGHT UNDERWEAR AND CLOTHES

So that there are no traces left at the place where we will do the tattoo. It will be more comfortable for both of us to work if you come in loose and soft underwear/clothes.

DO NOT DRINK ALCOHOL

At least 1 day before session (and after session as well). For me it’s very important that your body is not intoxicated and you feel fresh and well.

TRY NOT TO SPEND A LOT OF TIME IN THE SUN

Before our session, please don’t get a sunburn, because your skin can be irritated and it’s really hard to work on irritated and dry skin.

DON’T TAKE PAINKILLERS AT ALL

And don’t be scared if it’s your first tattoo, it’s really not as painful as you can think and being scared is not going to help us to make the best quality and adorable tattoo.
